NFL Brand

Brand Overview

The NFL brand is one of the most recognized in the world. These guidelines protect and amplify that identity across every partner, every platform, every market.

Brand Architecture

Brand Archetype: Hero & Magician

The NFL brand sits at the intersection of two powerful archetypes. The Hero archetype drives our athletic narratives — triumph, perseverance, excellence. The Magician archetype powers our entertainment and experience story — transformation, spectacle, and the impossible made real.

Every brand expression should activate one or both of these archetypes. Ask: does this feel like a championship moment, or a moment of pure NFL magic?

Brand Personality

The NFL brand voice is bold, inclusive, and authentic. It speaks with authority but without arrogance. It celebrates individual excellence in service of team. It reflects America's diversity while celebrating shared passion.

Tone descriptors: Passionate · Powerful · Unifying · Authentic · Celebratory

Brand Mission

We unite people and inspire communities in the joy of the game.

This mission drives every brand decision — from the colors we choose to the photography we license. If a creative execution doesn't unify or inspire, it doesn't belong in the NFL brand system.

Core Values

Respect — For the game, for teammates, for the fan.

Integrity — Consistency and honesty in every execution.

Responsibility to Team — Every brand action reflects on the whole NFL.

Resiliency — The brand endures and evolves while staying true to its roots.

The NFL Shield

The Most Recognized Shield in Sports

The NFL Shield is not just a logo — it's a promise. Its presence signals authenticity, quality, and the full weight of the NFL brand. Treat it accordingly.

Approved versions: Full color RGB (digital), full color CMYK (print), all-white (reversed on dark), all-black (single-color applications). No other colorways are approved.

Minimum sizes: 0.75 inch / 54px in any application. Below this, use NFL wordmark only.

Clear space: Minimum clear space equal to the width of the "N" in NFL on all sides at all times.

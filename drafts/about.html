The NFL Shield

The NFL Shield has become an enduring symbol because of the values it represents and the tradition it evokes. Few sports or entertainment identities have ever been so deeply woven into the fabric of American culture.

As the primary visual identifier of the NFL, it is essential that the NFL Shield is never altered in any way. In all communications — print, broadcast, and digital — the NFL Shield must always be highly visible and unmistakable.

Our Mission

We are all stewards of football. We unite people and inspire communities in the joy of the game by delivering the world's most exciting sports and entertainment.

Respect

Everyone matters. Everyone contributes. We celebrate diverse opinions and perspectives. We honor hard work and commitment.

Integrity

We always look to make the right call. We demand accountability and we expect fair play. We say what we mean, and always follow through.

Responsibility

As a team, we support one another. No matter how much we accomplish, no one is bigger than the game. We humbly strive to make our communities proud.

Resiliency

We set high standards and continuously strive for excellence. No matter how great the obstacles, we overcome adversity and rise to meet them.
